06 — Working Style

How We Work

🌅
Daily Check-ins
Quick async standups every morning — what we did, what we plan, any blockers. No meeting longer than 15 min.
🖥️
Pair Programming
For tough problems, two heads are better than one. We pair up to push through bugs and design decisions.
🔍
Code Reviews
Every PR gets at least one review. We treat reviews as learning moments, not gatekeeping. Constructive, always.
📺
Weekly Demos
Every Friday, we demo what we built — even if it's messy. Progress over perfection.
📚
Shared Learning
Interesting article? New framework? We drop it in our group thread. Knowledge shared is knowledge doubled.
🎯
Sprint Goals
Two-week mini-sprints with defined goals. Small, achievable milestones to keep momentum and celebrate wins.
